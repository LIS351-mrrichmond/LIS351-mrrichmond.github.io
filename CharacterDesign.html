My Character Designs

Character design is one of my favorite art forms no matter what medium I'm using. For these particular designs I used Adobe Photoshup. These characters are meant to exist in a fantasy-themed racing board game concept that I have been working on. Below is a list of their names and in-game abilities.

Peppy the Wizard
- Non-magic users aren't sure why, but the odds always seem to be in his favor. Odd dice rolls are doubled.
Aimar the Elven
- Competitors watch their backs when he's around. Long-range attack abilities.
Tridon the Water Nymph
- The fastest swimmer in all the lands. Skips over water spaces.
